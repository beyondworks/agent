### Scene 1: Dawn Market Light

**Duration:** 30 seconds

**Prompt:**
A 76-year-old Korean woman, 155cm tall, thin build, white hair neatly tied back in a short bun, deep wrinkles on her face but kind gentle eyes, rough weathered hands with calluses. Wearing a dark navy apron over a gray cotton t-shirt and black rubber shoes. She stands behind a stainless steel counter in a tiny kimbap shop, rolling kimbap with trembling hands, occasionally glancing at a wall calendar with a red circle drawn on today's date. Korean drama cinematography, warm golden tones mixed with cool fluorescent light inside the shop. The shop is framed through the half-open metal shutter, the dark empty market alley visible outside with all other shops closed. Shot on 50mm lens, shallow depth of field focusing on her weathered hands, the calendar softly blurred in the background. Predawn silence, a single fluorescent tube buzzes overhead, casting stark light on the ingredients — rice, pickled radish, spinach lined up neatly on the steel counter.

The camera starts on an extreme close-up of her trembling fingers rolling kimbap, then slowly pulls back through the shutter gap to reveal the lone lit shop in the dark alley.

Photorealistic, cinematic color grading, natural lighting, 4K, film grain, shallow depth of field.

**Audio Direction:**
Predawn silence. Distant truck rumbling. Faint fluorescent hum. A gentle, sparse piano melody begins — single notes, unhurried.

**Consistency Notes:**
This is the OPENING shot. Scene 20 must mirror this exact composition (same camera angle, same shop, same action) to create a circular narrative structure. Establish the warm-cool contrast: warm skin tones vs cool fluorescent light.
